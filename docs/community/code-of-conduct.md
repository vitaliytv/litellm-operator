Our community is dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users.

- **Be respectful** - Treat all community members with respect and kindness

- **Be professional** - Keep discussions focused and productive

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates our code of conduct, please report it to the maintainers at [security@yourdomain.com](mailto:security@yourdomain.com).

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
